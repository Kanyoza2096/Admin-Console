CORE PHILOSOPHY

The UI must answer three questions instantly:

1. What is happening?
2. What needs attention?
3. What should I do next?

Everything else is secondary.

Every animation, panel, chart and notification must exist for a reason.

No decorative widgets.

Every pixel communicates system state.
